Gyoza

About Gyoza

The key characteristic of gyoza (餃子) lies in its cooking method, which involves both pan-frying and steaming. They are first fried in a hot pan until crispy brown on the bottom sides, then a small amount of water is added before the pan is covered to quickly steam the entire dumplings. This technique gives gyoza the best mix of textures, where you get crispy bottoms and tender soft tops that encase the juicy filling inside.

Ingredients

  • 5 oz green cabbage (2-3 leaves)
  • 2 green onion/scallion (0.5 oz, 15 g)
  • 2 shiitake mushrooms
  • 3/4 lb ground pork
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp ginger
